A Bit of History...

Xvton started back in 2003 as a Singapore based startup company founded by Jarve Tan and Hans Lee. The goal was simple back then — to help companies build their websites. With the rapid advancement of technology, we converted to helping business with our very own artificial intelligence to mainly focus on analysing and projecting their growth in 2012. We hope to move the world towards a more technologically advanced future by helping with other tedious tasks, such as recommender systems and cybersecurity.
