Courale

Courale was a startup company that helped people with social anxiety deal with gatherings or in general, meet new people. It searches your approximate location with people with the same app and allow them to meetup and help each other when there's a need to. On the app there is also an option for people to become counsellors to those who need it. To put it simply, it is a social media app for those who have social anxiety and they needed to sort their data before embarking on designing their app.

Tecerch

Machine learning has become an integral part of the operations of many oil companies, allowing them to gather large volumes of information in real-time and translate data sets into actionable insights. This is was no different for Tecerch. Tecerch aimed to make oil more eco-friendly to combat climate change caused by carbon emissions. They view data as an extremely valuable resource, and they trusted their resources with Xvton. Saving time, reducing costs, boosting efficiencies, and improving safety are all crucial outcomes that they hoped to acheieve.

Farive

Technologies powered by machine learning can capture, analyze, and use data to personalize the shopping experience. This was exactly what Farive needed. They hope that their products can be more targetted at young adults as their products fit them. Here at Xvton, we programme our algorithms to find similarites and differences in customer data to simplify segmentation for precised targeting. Most importantly, we do not save personal data, but temporarily use it to present online shoppers with personalized recommendations by adjusting prices, coupons, and other incentives simultaneously.
